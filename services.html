Services

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is a profession dedicated to improving physical function and mobility through techniques like exercises and manual therapy. Whether recovering from injury or managing chronic conditions, physiotherapists work to alleviate pain and enhance well-being.

Intramuscular Stimulation

Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS) is a targeted therapy using fine needles to release muscle tension and alleviate pain by stimulating trigger points. Our skilled physiotherapists integrate IMS into personalized treatment plans to enhance muscle function and address chronic pain effectively.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture involves inserting thin needles at specific body points to restore balance and promote natural healing. Widely used for pain management and overall well-being, it is considered safe when administered by licensed practitioners.

Radial Shockwave Therapy

Experience healing innovation with Radial Shockwave Therapy. Our physiotherapists use acoustic waves to target and reduce pain effectively. Whether recovering from injury or enhancing mobility, our non-invasive approach brings advanced technology to your healing journey.

Personal Training

Achieve your fitness goals with personalized trainingt. Our expert trainers offer tailored workouts and motivation to suit your unique needs. Whether you're a beginner or aiming to push your limits, our one-on-one sessions guide you toward a stronger, healthier you.

Treatment for TMJ Dysfunction

Experience relief from TMJ discomfort. Our personalized treatment includes gentle exercises, manual techniques,and custom oral appliances if necessary. Whether it's jaw pain, headaches, or restricted movement, we address the root cause for effective relief.

Myofascial Release and Craniosacral Therapy

Myofascial Release (MFR) and Craniosacral Therapy are gentle manual techniques addressing musculoskeletal issues and promoting well-being. MFR targets fascia for improved mobility, while Craniosacral Therapy optimizes cerebrospinal fluid flow, supporting the central nervous system.

Sports Therapy

Sports therapy is a specialized field of healthcare focused on preventing, treating, and rehabilitating sports-related injuries. Sports therapists work with athletes to optimize performance, address injuries, and promote overall well-being through tailored interventions and injury prevention strategies.

What To Expect

At a physiotherapy appointment, expect a thorough assessment of your medical history and current symptoms. The physiotherapist will conduct a physical examination to diagnose the source of any issues. Based on the assessment, you'll receive a personalized treatment plan that may include hands-on therapy, exercises, and self-management guidance. Follow-up appointments will be scheduled to monitor your progress and adjust the plan as needed.
