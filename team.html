Therapists

Kendra Mulligan BScPT, CGIMS, FCAMPT

Kendra graduated from the University of Alberta in 1993 ans she worked in Northern Alberta for 18 years. In 2013, Kendra completed her FCAMPT certification - an internationally accredited program for utilizing clinical reasoning, exercise prescription, and manual therapy. In 2021, Kendra completed her Level 1 Foundations of Anatomical Acupuncture and she has been certified in GunnIMS (dry needling) since 2007. Kendra has co-owned Ascent Physiotherapy in Comox, BC since 2010 with Ron Mulligan.

Trish Leslie BScPT

Trish graduated from Queen Margaret University in Edinburgh with a Bachelor's of Science in Physical Therapy, with honours, in 2011. She has worked as a physiotherapist in orthopaedics, women's health, geriatrics, neurology and sports physiotherapy, as well as with rugby players. Trish has worked in private practice and acute care since 2012. Through her practice she focuses on manual therapies, physiotherapy modalities and exercise prescription. She is also certified in acupuncture from Acupuncture Foundation Canada Institute.

Jared Hromika MPT, FDN/IMS

Jared graduated from the University of British Columbia in 2014 with a Masters of Physical Therapy. Before moving to the Valley, he worked at several locum positions in private practice orthopaedics in order to gain exposure to different patient populations and approaches to treatment. Jared's approach to treatment involves movement assessment, manual therapy techniques, and exercise prescription. He also has additional training with Neurokinetic Therapy. Jared is excited to provide Ascent Physiotherapy with bike fitting services.

Shadi Fleifel MPT, FDN/IMS

Shadi has been part of the Ascent Physiotherapy Team since 2018. He graduated with a bachelor's degree in Kinesiology at Western University in London Ontario, before going to Perth, Australia to complete his Master's degree in Physiotherapy in 2008. He has worked with high level sports teams including the Canada Rugby 7's team in Victoria BC. Shadi is passionate about sports rehabilitation. Shadi is dedicated to continuing his education, and has participated in several courses to improve his Physiotherapy skills.

Chelsey de la Rey MPT

Chelsey completed her Bachelor of Human Kinetics degree at Trinity Western University in Langley before graduating from the University of British Columbia with her Masters of Physical Therapy in 2019. . She aims to provide comprehensive assessments and treatment. Chelsey has completed her Orthopedic Division Level 1 and looks forward to pursuing further education in manual therapy, pain science and active rehabilitation. She has enjoyed working with varsity athletes as a student athletic therapist and strength coach.

Personal Trainer

Carter Mulligan

Carter is a Registered BCRPA (BC Recreation and Parks Association) Fitness Leader with a Personal Training, Weight Training and Fitness Theory Certification. Carter has a personal interest in Bodybuilding and has been training extensively for the last 3 years. He previously competed competitively in lacrosse and rugby. He has been employed in the rehabilitation setting for the last year helping veterans, injured and recovering clients, and weekend warriors achieve their fitness, strength and functional goals.
